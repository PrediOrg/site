Know About Predi

Trending Creative Platform

Predi is a community-driven prediction market contract framework aimed at providing users with diverse prediction experiences across various domains like cryptocurrency prices, political elections, and sports events. With integrated DAO governance, Predi ensures fair and transparent platform operation, allowing community participation in project decisions. Users can convert their analytical skills into earnings through Predi's incentive model, which combines prediction rewards and activity mining.
